WASH AND DRY

Just like the normal laundry process at home, we wash and dry clothes using laundry machine and hold them afterwards. If your items are delicate, we have a hand wash service. We accept clothes like delicate lingerie, wool sweaters, and silk sweaters that need gentle care, and clothes with tough stains that you cannot easily be removed.

DRY CLEANING

Dry cleaning is a method of cleaning clothes and textiles without using water. Instead, a solvent is used to remove dirt, stains, and odors from the fabric. The most common solvent used in dry cleaning is perchloroethylene (perc), which is a colorless, nonflammable, and petroleum-based liquid.

Leather Cleaning

Leather cleaning in laundry involves the process of removing dirt, grime, and other impurities from leather clothing and accessories. Leather is a delicate material that requires special care and attention to maintain its quality and appearance. The cleaning method used for leather depends on the type of leather, the level of soiling, and the specific product being cleaned.

Ironing Service

Ironing is a method of pressing clothes to remove wrinkles and restore their original shape and appearance. The process involves heating an iron to a high temperature and then pressing it onto the fabric. The heat and pressure from the iron help to smooth out the fibers, making the fabric look neater and more presentable.
